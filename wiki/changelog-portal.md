# 🗂️ Portal changelog

What's changed in the **Server Assistant portal** — the web dashboard you sign into
with your Discord account. This page covers the portal experience itself; for the
bot's release notes see the [SA Bot changelog]({{ '/changelog/' | relative_url }}),
and for the Crest-collecting game see the
[Crestbound changelog]({{ '/wiki/changelog-crestbound/' | relative_url }}).

---

## 2026 — Crestbound joins the portal {#xp-games}

- **🎮 XP Games on the portal.** The portal home now has an **XP Games** section,
  with **[Crestbound]({{ '/wiki/crestbound/' | relative_url }})** — the Crest-collecting
  game — as its first entry. Open it to jump straight into your collection.
- **My Collection.** A new full-set view shows every Crest in the current set, with
  the ones you've found in full and the ones you haven't as placeholders, plus your
  spendable XP and a set-completion counter at the top.

## 2026 — Always up to date {#auto-update}

- **Automatic updates.** The portal now refreshes itself in the background, so you
  always see the latest version without needing to hard-reload the page.
- **Wiki link in the footer.** A link to the [Server Assistant Wiki]({{ '/wiki/' | relative_url }})
  now sits in the portal footer, so the full feature and command reference is always
  one tap away.

## 2026 — The customer portal {#portal-launch}

- **Sign in with Discord.** The customer portal launched as a web dashboard you reach
  by signing in with your Discord account — a home for the parts of Server Assistant
  that live on the web rather than in chat.
